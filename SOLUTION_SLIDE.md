# PROPOSED SOLUTION

*   **AI-Native Financial OS**: A "Fractional CFO" that unifies Bank, GST, and Invoice data into one ledger.
*   **Single Source of Truth**: Eliminates data silos, giving founders real-time visibility into cash flow and runway.
*   **From Invisible to Investable**: Generates verifiable credit scores from raw data, enabling instant loan underwriting.
*   **Multi-Agent Intelligence**: Specialized AI agents (Tax, Collections) work together to automate complex financial tasks.
*   **Digitized Trust**: Shifts lending from collateral-based to flow-based, unlocking capital for asset-light SMEs.
