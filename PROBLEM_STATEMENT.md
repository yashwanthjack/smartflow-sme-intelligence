# Problem Statement

### 1. Clearly Define the Problem
*   **The Data Black Box:** SME financial data is trapped in silos (Bank Statements, GST, Invoices).
*   **The Trust Deficit:** Using paper trails instead of digital proof makes real-time underwriting impossible.

### 2. Who Faces This Problem?
*   **63M SMEs:** Viable businesses rejected for loans because they are "Credit Invisible."
*   **Lenders:** Banks sitting on capital but unable to lend due to high verification costs.

### 3. Why Is It Important?
*   **Survival:** **40% of failures** are due to avoidable cash flow mismanagement, not lack of demand.
*   **Economy:** A **₹25 Lakh Crore ($300B)** credit gap exists simply because trust cannot be digitized.
