# FUTURE SCOPE & VISION

*   **Live Banking Integration**: We will connect to the Account Aggregator framework for regulated, real-time financial data fetching.
*   **Corporate Cards**: Issuing virtual expense cards will allow us to automate spending tracking and categorization.
*   **Mobile First Experience**: Launching a "CFO in your Pocket" app will enable founders to approve expenses on the go.
*   **B2B Marketplace**: We plan to build a curated platform for pre-approved loans and insurance, monetizing via commissions.
*   **Autonomous Finance**: Moving from advice to action, our agents will auto-invest idle cash to optimize yield.
*   **The Full-Stack OS**: Our end game is to replace 5 fragmented tools (Bank, Accounting, Taxes, Payroll, Analytics) with one unified platform.
